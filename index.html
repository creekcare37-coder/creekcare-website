Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Find quick details about our services below.

Do you bring your own tools and equipment?

Yes, we bring all of our own tools and equipment required to complete the job, including lawnmowers, trimmers, and safety gear.

What happens if it rains on my scheduled day?

If it rains on your scheduled day, we will contact you to reschedule your service to our very next available dry day.

We accept both Cash and e-Transfer for your convenience. Payment details will be provided upon completion of the service.
