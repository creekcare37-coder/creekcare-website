Safe and Thorough Gutter Cleaning Services

Clogged gutters can cause serious damage to your home, including roof leaks, rotted fascia boards, and water damage to your foundation. In Findlay Creek, Blossom Park, Hunt Club, Hunt Club Park, and Greenboro, mature trees drop leaves, pine needles, and seeds that quickly fill up gutters and clog downspouts. Our student team at CreekCare provides safe, thorough, and highly affordable gutter cleaning and inspection services to keep water flowing away from your home.

We clear out all dirt, leaves, and blockages by hand or with specialized pressure washing wands, ensuring a spotless clean. After clearing the horizontal gutters, we run water down every downspout to confirm that there are no hidden blocks in the elbow joints. During the job, we inspect the condition of your gutters—checking for loose hangers, rust spots, or leaks—and provide you with a full update on the system's condition.

What is Included:

  • Complete removal of leaves, dirt, moss, and debris from all gutters.
  • Thorough water flushing of all downspouts to ensure free drainage.
  • Visual inspection of gutter brackets, slope alignment, and seams for wear or leak issues.
  • Safe debris disposal—we bag and clean up everything so no mess is left in your yard.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I clean my gutters in Ottawa?

We recommend cleaning your gutters at least twice a year, once in the late spring and once in the late fall, to prevent leaves and ice dams from blocking the flow.

Do you flush the downspouts as part of the service?

Yes, we flush every downspout with water after clearing debris to make sure the entire gutter system is flowing freely.

Do you inspect for gutter damage?

Yes, we inspect all brackets, joints, and alignments during the cleaning process and will alert you to any leaks, sagging, or issues we find.
