Clutter-Free Garage Cleanouts & Organization

Garages easily become dumping grounds for old boxes, broken appliances, seasonal decor, and garden tools, eventually leaving no room to park your car. Clearing out years of clutter takes a lot of time and heavy lifting. Our student team at CreekCare provides fast, reliable, and affordable garage cleanout and junk removal services in Findlay Creek and Blossom Park Ottawa.

We work with you to sort items into keep, donate, recycle, and trash piles. Our team handles all the heavy lifting, carrying items out, loading them for hauling, and sorting them responsibly. Once the clutter is cleared, we sweep down the garage floor, wipe down surfaces, and can help you set up storage shelves to organize the items you decide to keep. Reclaim your garage space today!

What is Included:

  • Sorting clutter into clear donation, recycling, garbage, and keep categories.
  • All heavy lifting, carrying, and loading of junk items.
  • Hauling and responsible disposal—donating usable items to local Ottawa charities.
  • Complete sweep-out and cleanup of the garage floor and surfaces.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you charge for garage cleanouts?

Our pricing depends on the volume of items to be cleared and the time required. We provide a clear, upfront estimate before starting any work.

What items can you not take?

We cannot haul away hazardous chemicals, paint cans, propane tanks, or asbestos. We can help you sort them for Ottawa's safe municipal drop-off sites.

Do you donate or recycle usable items?

Yes, we sort through all clutter and separate items that can be donated to local Ottawa charities or recycled, minimizing landfill waste.
