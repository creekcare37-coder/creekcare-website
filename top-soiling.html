Premium Topsoil Spreading & Lawn Dressing

Over time, residential lawns lose nutrients, compact, and develop bumpy, uneven spots due to foot traffic, pets, and weather. Topsoiling (often paired with overseeding) is one of the most effective ways to restore vital organic matter, fill low spots, and stimulate thick, green grass growth. CreekCare provides fast, reliable, and affordable topsoil spreading services across Ottawa South in Ottawa.

Our student landscapers screen and deliver topsoil directly to your property, wheelbarrow it to the target zones, and hand-rake it to ensure a thin, even layout. We take care to maintain standard yard grading so water runs away from your home's foundation. Whether you want to top-dress your lawn before summer or add premium organic soil to raise new flower beds, we handle all the heavy wheelbarrowing and raking work at flat, student-budget rates.

What is Included:

  • Delivery and manual transport of premium screened organic topsoil.
  • Thin, uniform spreading of soil across target lawn zones or garden beds.
  • Minor hand-grading and raking to fill low points, holes, and level bumpy areas.
  • Clean-up of driveways, walkways, and curbs after soil application.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I add topsoil to my lawn?

Spreading a thin layer of topsoil (top dressing) adds organic matter and nutrients back into your lawn. It helps fill in uneven patches, improves water retention, and creates a healthy environment for grass roots to grow thick.

When is the best time for topsoiling?

Late spring and early fall are the ideal times for topsoiling. This aligns with active grass growing seasons, which allows overseeded grass seeds to germinate and establish successfully.

What kind of soil do you use?

We source high-quality, screened organic loam topsoil from trusted local suppliers. It is rich in organic nutrients and free of large stones, clay clumps, or weeds.
