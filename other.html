Got a different chore or project? Inquire with us!

Have an outdoor chore, clean-up task, or yard project that doesn't fit into our standard service list? No problem! At CreekCare, we are a team of hard-working, energetic local students ready to tackle odd jobs, heavy lifting, and seasonal outdoor helper tasks. We love helping neighbors cross chores off their to-do lists.

Simply use our booking contact form to let us know exactly what you need done. We will review your custom project scope, confirm if we have the equipment and capacity to handle it, and get back to you with a transparent, no-obligation quote. If it's a job that requires heavy machinery or licensed specialists (such as electrical, plumbing, or large tree climbing), we will let you know honestly and point you in the right direction.

Examples of custom tasks we can handle:

  • Heavy lifting, moving soil/mulch piles, and wheelbarrowing assistance.
  • Lawn and garden weeding, soil aeration, and flower bed maintenance.
  • Pruning and trimming of small bushes, hedges, or low-hanging branches.
  • Yard debris cleanup, organizing garden sheds, or sweeping patios and driveways.
  • Seasonal setup/take-down of patio furniture, gazebos, or basic yard decorations.
