Professional Trash & Compost Bin Washing

Dirty garbage and organic compost bins attract pests like raccoons, wasps, and flies, and build up harmful bacteria and foul smells over time. Cleaning them out by hand is an unpleasant and difficult chore. Our student team at CreekCare offers a fast, professional, and eco-friendly trash bin cleaning service in Ottawa South to keep your bins sanitized and fresh.

We wash, disinfect, and deodorize your bins right at your curb. Our team uses high-pressure spray wands combined with biodegradable sanitizing formulas that kill bacteria, viruses, and insect larvae. We schedule the cleaning to align with your neighborhood's garbage collection day, washing the bins right after they are emptied so you never have to deal with the odor or grime again.

What is Included:

  • Deep pressure washing of garbage, recycling, and organic green bins.
  • Eco-friendly disinfection killing 99.9% of germs and bacteria.
  • Deodorizing treatment using natural formulas to leave a clean, fresh scent.
  • Rinsing and washing the exterior surfaces and handles for total hygiene.

Frequently Asked Questions

When do you perform bin cleaning?

We typically schedule bin cleaning on your city waste collection day (or the day after) so your bins are completely empty and ready to be washed.

What cleaning solutions do you use?

We use high-pressure hot water and biodegradable, eco-friendly sanitizers that kill 99% of bacteria and eliminate odors without harming your lawn.

Can you clean green organic compost bins?

Yes, we wash and sanitize green organic bins, blue bins, black bins, and standard gray garbage bins.
