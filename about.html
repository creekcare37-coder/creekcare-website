Your Hardworking Neighborhood Student Team

CreekCare was founded by a team of motivated, local students with a simple mission: to provide our neighbors in Findlay Creek and Blossom Park with reliable, high-quality yard and home maintenance services at prices that make sense. We realized that many homeowners need help with tasks like lawn mowing, gutter clearing, or sorting garage clutter, but don't want to pay the inflated prices of large corporate contractors.

As students, we bring energy, focus, and a strong work ethic to every job. By keeping our operations local and efficient, we are able to pass significant savings on to our clients. We treat every yard and home we work on as if it were our own, paying attention to the small details that make a big difference.

Our Core Values

1. Reliability Above All

When we schedule a job, we show up on time and ready to work. If unexpected weather like heavy rain causes a delay, we communicate immediately and reschedule you to our very next available dry day.

2. Real Affordability

We keep our prices fair and transparent. Home maintenance shouldn't break the bank, and we make sure you receive high-quality student service without any hidden costs.

3. Caring for the Community

We live and go to school in the community we serve. Building long-term relationships with our neighbors is incredibly important to us, which is why we always guarantee friendly service and clean results.
