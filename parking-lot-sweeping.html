Professional Commercial & Residential Lot Sweeping

Sand, gravel, leaves, and trash make parking lots look neglected and create slippery safety hazards for clients, vehicles, and pedestrians. CreekCare offers thorough and affordable commercial and residential parking lot sweeping services throughout Ottawa South, covering Findlay Creek, Blossom Park, Hunt Club, Hunt Club Park, and Greenboro.

Using our professional Kubota utility tractors fitted with front-mounted heavy-duty rotary sweepers, we efficiently clear accumulated winter gravel, sand, dirt, and leaves from commercial plazas, office parkings, and townhome roads. Our student crew detail-sweeps curbs, corners, walkways, and dumpster surrounds with commercial blowers and steel brooms, leaving your entire property clean and professional. We offer one-time spring cleanups or recurring contract sweeping based on your needs.

What is Included:

  • Machine sweeping of all asphalt parking spaces, drive lanes, and entrances.
  • Power blowing of walkways, corner spaces, and hard-to-reach curb lines.
  • Manual cleanup around dumpster enclosures and loading docks.
  • Collection, bagging, and neat staging or disposal of sand and litter.

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of properties do you sweep?

We sweep commercial retail plazas, office building parking lots, underground garages, residential townhome complexes, and large private driveways.

Do you remove winter sand and gravel?

Yes, our team specializes in spring cleanup sweeping to clear accumulated winter sand, gravel, and salt debris from asphalt parking lots and walkways.

What equipment do you use?

We use professional Kubota utility tractors fitted with heavy front rotary sweepers, combined with commercial power backpack blowers and hand tools for detail-sweeping corners and curbs.
